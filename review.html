The Black Phone 1,2

The Black Phone is about a shy 13-year old boy named finney who is abducted by a sadistic killer called the Grabber. Trapped in a soundproof basement, Finney discovers a disconnected rotary phone rings and he can hear the ghosts of the Grabbers previous victims, who try to help him escape. His sister Gwen, uses her psychic dreams to try and find him, while Finney uses the advice from the ghosts on the phone to fight back against the killer.
